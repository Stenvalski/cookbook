Schnitzel and Leak salad

Schnitzel

Ingredients

  • Schnitzel meat (pork)
  • Salt & pepper
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Bread crumbs (rasp)
  • Oil + Butter
  • Lard

Instructions

  1. Hammer the meat to a consistent size and cut them so they fit inside the pan
  2. Dry the meat with a kitchen towel
  3. Add salt and pepper on both sides
  4. Turn the meat in flour until covered completely
  5. Visk the eggs and bath the meat in the egg-mixture
  6. Cover the meat in bread crumbs and set aside
  7. Heat the pan and enough oil or lard to at least cover the whole pan. It should preferably be half as deep as the meat is high
  8. Roast the meat until golden brown on both sides
  9. Let the meat rest a few minutes on a plate with kitchen towel

Leak salad

Ingredients

  • Leak
  • Apple
  • Lemon juice (teaspoon-ish)
  • Mayonnaise
  • Table spoon of sugar
  • Salt and pepper

instructions

  1. Wash the leak properly and cut it into thin slices
  2. Peel the apple and grate (coarsely)
  3. Mix the above with the lemon, sugar, salt and mayo
  4. Season with salt and pepper
