Carbonara

Recipe #1 Luciano Monosilio

  • Guanciale - 50g/person
  • Egg - 1 whole + 1 egg yolk/person
  • Pecorino - 5g/person
  • Grana Padano - 8g/person
  • Pepper - approximately 5g/person
  • Salt
  • Spagehettoni - 75g/person

Instructions

  1. From the guanciale, remove the layer of pepper as it has a tendency to burn
  2. Cut the guanciale into cubes approximately 1cm x 1cm x 1cm
  3. Fry the guanciale until the sides are golden.
  4. Boil the pasta in salted water until al dente
  5. Meanwhile mix the egg and egg yolks in a large bowl with the Pecorino and Grana Padano to a uniform sauce
  6. When the pasta is done, add it to the bowl together with the guanciale pieces and half the liquid fat
  7. Add most of the pepper and mix everthing together
  8. Plate and add a little extra cheese mix, pepper and carefully salf if needed

Recipe #2 Flavio di Maio

  • Guanciale - 50g/person
  • Egg - 1 whole + 1 egg yolk/person
  • Pecorino - 8g/person
  • Parmesan 5g/person
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • White wine
  • Spagehettoni - 75g/person or
  • Rigatoni 100g/person

Instructions

  1. Same procedure as above, except guanciale are cut smaller
  2. The wine is added to stop the frying of the guanciale
