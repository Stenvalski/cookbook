Guacamole

Ingredients

  • Avocados: The riper, the better.
  • Finely diced red onion. Wash the cut onions if you want less onion flavor
  • Cilantro / Coriander
  • Jalapeño / Chilli
  • Lime juice
  • Fine sea salt: A critical ingredient to bring out all of those good flavors!
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Ground cumin: It’s the secret ingredient that rounds out all of those flavors

Instructions

  1. Mash your avocados: Add them to a large mixing bowl and mash with a fork or spoon until they reach your desired consistency.
  2. Mix in the remaining ingredients: Add in the onion, cilantro, jalapeño / chilli, lime juice, salt, cumin, and stir until combined.
  3. Taste and season: Give the mixture a taste, and refine seasoning.
