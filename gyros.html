Gyros with Tzatziki

Tzatziki

Ingredients

  • 400 g Greek yogurt
  • 1 1/2 large cucumbers
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, finely grated
  • Salt and freshly ground pepper
  • 1 tbsp freshly squeezed lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Coarsely grate the cucumber and squeeze out as much moisture as possible.
  2. Place the cucumber in a sieve, season with salt—about the same amount you would add to the dressing—and toss with your hands. Let the cucumber sit for about 15 minutes, then squeeze out any excess moisture, preferably through a cloth.
  3. Meanwhile, mix the ingredients for the dressing - stir the yogurt together with the garlic, olive oil, and lemon juice.
  4. Then stir in the cucumbers and season the dressing with freshly ground pepper and more salt if needed.

Gyros seasoning

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon oregano
  • 1 tablespoon thyme
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 1 tablespoon ground coriander
  • 1 tablespoon dried parsley
  • 1/2 tablespoon paprika
  • 1/2 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Mix all the spices together and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Store the spice mixture in an airtight container or spice jar.

Gyros skewers

Ingredients

  • 6-700 g pork tenderloin
  • 3 tbsp gyros seasoning
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 3-4 tbsp Greek yogurt - preferably 10% fat
  • Salt and freshly ground pepper

Instructions

  1. Cut the tenderloin into long, thin slices/strips.
  2. You can also cut it into cubes, but the strips provide more surface area for the marinade to adhere to, resulting in deliciously moist skewers.
  3. Mix the meat with the spices, oil, and Greek yogurt, and toss well so that all surfaces are coated in the marinade.
  4. Season with salt and freshly ground pepper, but be aware if your gyro spice mix already contains salt.
  5. Then fold the meat onto the skewers, pressing it out so that the meat is not too tightly packed.
  6. If you are using wooden skewers, soak them in water for about 20 minutes to ensure they do not burn during cooking.
  7. Grill or fry the skewers in a little oil until they are golden brown and cooked through.
  8. The skewers can also be cooked in an air fryer for about 10-12 minutes at 200 degrees Celsius, or in the oven for about 10-12 minutes at 215 degrees Celsius.
