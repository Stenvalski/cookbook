Tuna Mousse

Ingredients

  • 2 cans Rio Mare tuna (in oil and with added pepperoncino)
  • 150g of Philadelphia cream cheese
  • A spoonful of Greek yogurt
  • A bunch of dill
  • Half a spoonful of french mustard
  • Half a spoonful of dijon mustard
  • Half a red onion
  • A few capers
  • Several small, or 2 larger pickled cucumbers
  • A little bit of sriracha sauce
  • Zest and juice of half a lemon
  • Salt and pepper
  • A large spoonful of grated horseradish
  • Optional ingredients:
  • 1 roasted beetroot
  • Rye bread with grains
  • A 7 minute egg
  • Grated horseradish

Instructions

  1. Drain the tuna from the excess oil and transfer it to a bowl. We add cream cheese, horseradish, mustard, Greek yogurt, zest and lemon juice as well as salt and pepper.
  2. Chop artichokes, capers, cornichokes and dill finely and add them to the bowl. I mix and season as necessary.
  3. Toast 2 slices of rye bread, I cut the beetroot on a mandolin, I cook the egg for 7 minutes. After this time I cool them in cold water and peel them. Spread the toast extensively with tuna paste, I put half an egg in the middle and slices of beetroot on it. I grate fresh horseradish on sandwiches.
