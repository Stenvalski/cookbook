Cranberry Brie Bites

Ingredients

  • 1 sheet of puff pastry
  • 1 Brie
  • 1 cup of cranberry jam or sauce
  • Fresh Rosemary

Instructions

  1. Cut the puff pastry in 12 equal size rectangles and place them in a muffin tray.
  2. Add a piece of brie in each and top of with cranbarry juice.
  3. Add them in the oven according the description (200C for 15min).
  4. Top of each bite with a little bit of fresh Rosemary
  5. Serve while warm to the touch
