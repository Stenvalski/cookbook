Chicken Nuggets

Marinade

  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 large tbsp. dried cumin
  • 1/3 tsp. dried chili or cayenne pepper
  • 1 tbsp. brown sugar or sugar
  • 1 large tsp. salt + a little pepper
  • 1/2 dl oil
  • 1/2 dl water
  • 1/2 dl flour
  • 2 large tbsp. tomato paste / ketchup

Rest

  • 600 g chicken breast
  • 1 1/25 dl panko
  • 2x 1/2 dl oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Grate the garlic into a bowl and add the remaining marinade ingredients and mix well.
  2. Cut your chicken breasts into approximately four pieces and toss them in the marinade
  3. Put panko in a bowl and turn the marinated chicken pieces in the panko to cover them completely
  4. Get out a frying pan and add 1/2 dl of oil, turn up the heat (7 out of 9)
  5. Now take the breaded chicken pieces and put them on the pan, turn them after 4 minutes. I was able to have about 6 pieces of chicken on the pan at first. They should be golden brown and crispy and the chicken should be cooked through without being dry. Keep an eye out, stoves work differently. So the 4 minutes on each side may not suit your stove.
  6. Once the first round is cooked, transfer the chicken nuggets to a plate with paper towels. Add another 1/2 dl of oil and fry the last 6 pieces.
