New York style cheesecake

Cake bottom

  • 250 g digestive cookies/ petit beurres / butter cookies
  • 125 g of butter, melted
  1. Blend the cookies to obtain a biscuit mass with the consistency of sand.
  2. Place the biscuits in the bowl, pour melted butter, stir with a spoon until it is like ‘wet sand’.
  3. Line a cake form (ø 22 - 23 cm) with baking paper, only the bottom.
  4. Distribute the cookie mass evenly, pat thoroughly into the bottom and sides of the form with a bottom of the spoon.
  5. Refrigerate for half an hour.
  6. Wrap the form twice with aluminium foil - the cheesecake will be baked in a water bath.

Filling

  • All ingredients should be at room temperature.
  • 750 g of Polish cheescake cheese (the best is PIĄTNICA twaróg sernikowy )
  • 3 eggs
  • 220 g of sugar
  • 180 g of thick sour cream 18%
  • 2 teaspoons of grated lemon peel
  • 60 ml of lemon juice
  1. Place all the ingredients in a bowl. DO NOT mix for too long, basically only to combine the ingredients
  2. (We don’t want to air the cheese filling unnecessarily. After baking, the cheesecake top should be straight).
  3. Pour the cheese filling onto the cooled bottom and distribute evenly.
  4. Bake at 170ºC in a water bath * for about 75 minutes or more. When ready the cheesecake should not be runny but rather a bit bouncy on top.
  5. Allow it to cool in a slightly open oven, then cool in the refrigerator for about 12 hours.
  6. * Bake the cheesecake in a water bath: put the cheesecake form into a larger form or a heat-resistant bowl, into which pour boiling water up to half the height of the cheese form.
  7. Store New York cheesecake in the refrigerator.
