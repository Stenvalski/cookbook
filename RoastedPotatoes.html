Roasted Potatoes

Ingredients

  • 1 pork tenderloin
  • salt, pepper, 1 tablespoon of dried thyme, 1/2 teaspoon of sweet pepper
  • 2 tablespoons of extra olive oil
  • 1 medium onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 30 ml of whiskey
  • 120ml broth
  • 120ml cream 30%+
  • spoon of chopped parsley

Instructions

  • Peel potatoes and cut to the final size.
  • Boil potatoes for about 10min or soft on the outside.
  • Turn on the oven to 220C

    • In the meantime prepare the rosemary garlic oil:
      • Cut rosemary into small pieces.
      • Crush a few garlic cloves with a garlic crushers.
      • Warm a few table spoons of olive oil together with the rosemary and garlic until the garlic turns golden.
      • Strain the oil into a big bowl (make sure itâ€™s not too much for the amount of potatoes you have).
      • Keep the rosemary and garlic for later.

  • Strain out the water and let them steam off.
  • Transfer the potatoes to the bowl.
  • Add salt and pepper.
  • Toss the potatoes until the side becomes mushy.
  • Transfer the potatoes to the backing tray and put then into the oven.
  • Let the potatoes roast for 30min or until the potatoes have a golden brown color on the underside.

    • While the potatoes are roasting prepare garlic mayonnaise for dipping:
      • Add mayo, garlic powder, pepper, salt and other herbs to a glass.
      • Add a bit of water and mix.

  • Turn up the heat (around 300C) and activate the grill function.
  • Let the potatoes roast for 3-4min or until the top side has a similar color as the bottom. Keep an eye on them.
  • Transfer the potatoes back to the bowl, add the rosemary & garlic and toss.
