Root Vegetable Salad

Salad

    2 portions
  • 300 g of beetroots
  • 300 g sweet potatoes
  • 2 big carrots
  • 1 red onion
  • 1.5 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 50 g of nuts or pumpkin seeds
  • 2 handfuls of rucola
  • a pinch of chili flakes
  • optional 80 g of feta or brie cheese
  • 1 avocado
  • 1/2 pomegranate

Dressing

  • 2 teaspoons of honey or maple syrup
  • 1 tablespoon of extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of lemon juice
  • optional: 1 tablespoon of tahini or 2 teaspoons of mustard

Instructions

  1. Heat the oven to 200 degrees C.
  2. Peel the beetroots, carrots and sweet potatoes, cut them into cubes (sweet potatoes small than rest). Peel the onion and cut into 1/8s.
  3. Place the vegetables on a baking tin, season with salt and pepper and drizzle with 1 tablespoon of olive oil. Put in the oven and bake for 25-30 minutes.
  4. Take the baking tray out of the oven, toss the vegetables, let them cool down. Peel the avocado, remove the stone and cut into pieces, add to the baked vegetables.
  5. Add rucola on the top, roasted nuts or pumpkin seeds, sprinkle with chili and drizzle with the remaining olive oil. You can also add cubes of feta cheese or blue cheese.
  6. Sprinkle with pomegranate.
  7. Mix the ingredients for the sauce and pour over the salad
