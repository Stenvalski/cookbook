Beer braised Beefstew

Beer braised beefstew with crushy root vegetables (4 pers)

Ingredients

  • 200g bacon
  • 500g cubed beef (+ 1/2 dl flour and salt/pepper)
  • 2 onions, coarsely diced
  • 2 carrots, coarsely diced
  • 1 dark beer, 33cl
  • 2 1/2 dl water + 1 beef bouillon cube
  • 4-5 bay leaves
  • 750g mixed root vegetables, peeled and coarsely diced (e.g. parsnips, yellow beets, carrots, Jerusalem artichoke etc.)
  • 2 dl pearl onions

Instructions

  1. Attention!!: Add the root vegetables after 1.5-2h
  2. Fry the bacon crispy in a pan. Remove the bacon but leave the fat.
  3. Toss the beef tenderloin in flour, salt and pepper, then brown well in the pan. Pick up the meat.
  4. Saute the onion and carrots for 2 minutes and then put the bacon and meat back in the pan.
  5. Add beer, water, stock cube and bay leaves. Let simmer covered at low heat for 2 hours.
  6. Add root vegetables and pearl onions and simmer for another 30 minutes. Season to taste and serve with mashed potatoes.
