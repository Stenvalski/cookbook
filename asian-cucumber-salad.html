Asian cucumber salad

Ingredients

  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 small shallot
  • 2-3 spring onions
  • 3-4 tsp chilli crisps
  • 2 tsp of semane seeds
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil

Instructions

  1. Smash or cut the cucumber in half lengthwise and then slice it into mouth size pieces
  2. Place the cucumber in a food storage container and sprinkle with salt. Let sit for 10 minutes to draw out excess moisture
  3. Drain the cucumber and mix in the the remaining ingredients
  4. Shake violently so that the cucumber and ingredients get mixed well
  5. Taste and adjust seasoning
  6. Store in the fridge until serving
