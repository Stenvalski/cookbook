Rhubarb Strawberry Crumble

Ingredients

Crumble

  • 120 g of butter, melted
  • 100 g of sugar
  • 8 g of vanilla sugar
  • 100 g of wheat flour
  • 120 g of oatmeal

Filling

  • 400 g of strawberries
  • 400 g of rhurbarb
  • 1 tablspoon of potato starch

Instructions

  1. Crumble - Put all the ingredients in the dish. Knead between your fingers until you get the crumble, set aside.
  2. Wash and cut fruits to approximately 1cm sized cubes.
  3. Build the crumble with fruits at the bottom and the crumble on top
  4. Bake at 180ÂºC for about 35 minutes or until the crumble is golden brown. The fruit filling inside will boil noticeably. Take it out, cool it down for a moment. Can be served warm, for example with vanilla ice cream.
